# 🧠 AI Receptionist Dashboard (Dental-Focused)

## 🎯 Purpose
This dashboard is designed to clearly show **patients handled, appointments booked, and revenue generated**, not technical AI metrics.

---

# 🟩 1. Weekly Summary (Top Section)

**This Week at Your Practice**

- Patients contacted your clinic: XX  
- Appointments successfully scheduled: XX  
- Patients booked after hours: XX  
- Estimated revenue captured: $XXXX  

---

# 🟦 2. Patient Calls Handled

**Patient Inquiries Managed**

- Total patient calls received  
- Calls handled without staff  
- Missed calls recovered  
- Average call duration  

---

# 🟨 3. Appointments Booked

**Appointments We Secured for You**

- Total appointments booked  
- Booking rate (% of callers who booked)  
- Emergency appointments captured  
- Rescheduled appointments  

---

# 🟪 4. After-Hours Coverage

**Patients Captured Outside Working Hours**

- Calls handled after hours  
- Appointments booked after closing  
- Weekend calls handled  

---

# 🟥 5. Revenue Impact

**Estimated Revenue Recovered**

- Average patient value (editable)  
- Total appointments booked  
- Estimated revenue generated  

Formula:
`Appointments × Avg Patient Value = Revenue`

---

# 🟫 6. Patient Insights

**What Your Patients Are Asking**

- Top call reasons:
  - Emergency visits  
  - Cleaning  
  - Consultation  
- Frequently asked questions  
- Common service requests  

---

# 🟧 7. Call Breakdown

**Detailed Call Activity**

- Incoming vs missed calls  
- AI-handled vs staff-handled calls  
- Call volume by time (peak hours)  

---

# 🟨 8. Booking Funnel

**Call → Booking Flow**

- Total calls  
- Qualified inquiries  
- Appointments booked  
- Conversion rate (%)  

---

# 🟩 9. Staff Workload Reduction

**Front Desk Relief Metrics**

- Calls handled by AI (vs staff)  
- Estimated staff time saved  
- Peak-time call coverage  

---

# 🟦 10. Live Call Log

**Recent Patient Interactions**

- Caller name (if captured)  
- Call time  
- Outcome (Booked / Inquiry / Missed)  
- Notes / summary  

---

# 🟪 11. Performance Trends

**Weekly / Monthly Trends**

- Calls handled over time  
- Appointments booked over time  
- Revenue trend  

---

# 🟥 12. System Status

**AI Receptionist Health**

- System active status  
- Last active time  
- Integration status (calendar, CRM, etc.)  

---

# 🟫 13. Custom Settings

**Practice Controls**

- Average patient value (editable)  
- Business hours  
- Call handling preferences  
- Booking rules  

---

# 🔥 Core Philosophy

This dashboard focuses on:

- Patients handled  
- Appointments booked  
- Revenue generated  

NOT:

- technical AI metrics  
- backend complexity  

---

# ⚡ Key Rule

If a dentist cannot understand the dashboard in **10 seconds**,  
it is too complex.
