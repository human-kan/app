# 🧠 AI Receptionist Dashboard Upgrade Checklist

## 🎯 Goal
Transform dashboard from:
❌ AI tool interface  
➡️  
✅ Revenue + patient growth dashboard

---

# 🔴 CRITICAL ADDITIONS (HIGH PRIORITY)

## 1. Weekly Summary (TOP SECTION)

### Add a new top card:

**"This Week at Your Practice"**

- Patients handled: XX  
- Appointments booked: XX  
- After-hours bookings: XX  
- Estimated revenue generated: $XXXX  

👉 Must be the FIRST thing visible  
👉 Should explain value in <10 seconds  

---

## 2. ROI Display

### Add:

- Monthly revenue generated  
- Cost of service ($500)  
- ROI multiple  

Example:

`You paid: $500 → Generated: $5,700 → ROI: 11.4x`

---

## 3. After-Hours Impact

### Add a dedicated section:

**"After-Hours Patient Capture"**

- Calls handled after closing  
- Appointments booked after hours  
- Revenue from after-hours  

---

## 4. Missed Call Recovery

### Add:

**"Missed Calls We Recovered"**

- Missed calls captured  
- Bookings from missed calls  
- Revenue from recovered calls  

---

## 5. Front Desk Relief

### Add:

**"Front Desk Work Reduced"**

- Calls handled by AI  
- Estimated staff time saved (hours)  
- Peak-time call handling  

---

# 🟠 LANGUAGE FIXES (VERY IMPORTANT)

## Replace technical terms:

| Current | Replace With |
|--------|-------------|
| Revenue Capture Rate | % of callers who booked |
| Value Funnel | Patients → Appointments → Revenue |
| Staff Insulation | Front Desk Work Reduced |
| Calls handled | Patients handled |
| Booked (+$100) | New Patient Booked ($100 revenue) |

---

## Rule:
👉 Every metric must connect to:
- patients  
- appointments  
- revenue  

---

# ⚠️ DESIGN RULES

## Keep:

- Clean UI  
- Minimal clutter  
- Dark theme  

---

## Avoid:

❌ Technical AI metrics  
❌ Overloading charts  
❌ Complex wording

# 🧠 CORE PRINCIPLE

The dashboard should answer ONE question instantly:

👉 "How many patients and how much money did this bring me?"

---

# ⚡ FINAL CHECK

If a dentist cannot understand the value in **10 seconds**:

👉 The dashboard is wrong
